Gig'o'Books DO's and DON'Ts

DO use Gig'o'Books IF:

  • You are doing something by yourself and you have simple affairs.
  • You want something that's tailored to your niche, is simple, and without unnecessary clutter.
  • You need to create tax-compliant invoices with a minimum of fuss.
  • You need to create regular tax reports for your local tax/revenue authority with a minimum of fuss.
  • You use spreadsheets (ie. Excel, Google Sheets) or a cashbook to do your accounting and wonder if there's something better.
  • You LOVE free!
  • You LOVE great customer support straight from the developers.
  • You resent artificial restrictions that push you into paying more money.
  • You don't want your data to be in the 'cloud' and locked-in by the vendor.
  • You want something that's open source and will keep working until you retire.
  • You need to handle multiple currencies.
  • You need double-entry accounting. (If you don't know what this is, then don't worry.)
  • You are privacy conscious.
  • You don't have employees (but having contractors is okay).
  • You don't have to keep track of inventory/stock (unless it's only a small amount).

DON'T use Gig'o'Books IF:

  • You have employees or you have to do payroll.
  • You have to keep track of inventory/stock (unless it's only a small amount).
  • You have complicated financial affairs.
  • Someone else works on the accounts at the same time as you.
  • You need automation or integration with third party services.
  • You like spending money unnecessarily.
  • You like having to pay money monthly so your data doesn't get deleted, or
  • You like having to pay for updates every few years so you can continue to access your data.
  • You like having features that you never use.
  • You like having features that you don't understand.

If you are a DO-er, then Gig'o'Books has been tailored especially for you. It's your best choice.

Multi currency

{% include hs-toggle.html id="multi-currency-f" %}

Gig'o'Books has true multi-currency support. You can enter transactions in any currency.

However, sales and purchases can only entered in a single currency (out of the currencies which are configured). For example, you can have an invoice that is in USD and another invoice which is in EUR, but you can't have an invoice which contains a USD amount and a EUR amount at the same time.

Reports can contain multiple currencies (where applicable), or can be configured to convert all amounts to a single currency (where applicable).

FAQs

{% include hs-checkbox.html id="data-trust-q" %} {% include hs-toggle.html id="data-trust-q" %} Can I trust you with looking after my data? How do you protect my data?

The beautiful answer to this question is: There's no need to!

Unlike other cloud-based or SaaS services, your file is not stored with us. Instead, it's stored on your own computer or phone.

Or if you save your file to a cloud folder by using something like Dropbox or Google Drive, it's saved to your own cloud, not ours. There's nothing for us to protect.

{% include hs-checkbox.html id="cloud-storage-q" %} {% include hs-toggle.html id="cloud-storage-q" %} I want to store my file in the cloud. I want to use Gig'o'Books from any device. Can I do this?

Yes, you can!

Set up a cloud folder using something like Dropbox or Google Drive. Then, when you save your file, save it to the cloud folder.

Then, you can (use Gig'o'Books and) access the file from any device which has access to the folder.

(Speculation: In the future, we may consider having Gig'o'Books save directly to a user-provided cloud ie. Amazon S3.)
